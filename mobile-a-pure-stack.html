02   What it is

A full AI chat interface, on your G2s.

i · any model

ChatGPT. Claude. Gemini. Local.

Route to whichever model you prefer. Switch mid-conversation.

ii · two agents

OcuClaw and Even AI.

Different defaults for each. Different personalities on the same pair of glasses.

iii · your server

Runs on your machine.

Your keys. Your data. Your memory, persisted where you can see it.

ii

Open source.

OpenClaw on GitHub, permissive license. Read it. Fork it. Run it.

iii

Free forever.

Zero dollars. The only bill comes from model providers you chose.
